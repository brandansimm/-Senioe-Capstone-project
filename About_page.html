WHY WE CHOOOSE THIS PRODUCT

The purpose of this project is to form an information system that allows collegiate student athletics the opportunity to receive high quality tutoring services while they are travelling and competing at various campuses. We want to be able to aid student athletes in receiving the same education as other students, even if their schedule doesn’t allow for it sometimes, every student deserves an equal education Our mission statement is: “To provide college athletes with the proper resources and capabilities to ensure an uninterrupted education is attainable while traveling and competing in collegiate sports.”


Advantages

This information system will allow student athletes to access tutoring services while attending various campuses away from the MGSU campus. The students will have access to a diverse group of tutors who specialize in different subjects and concentrations. The AthletiStudy system will help them gain better learning skills and offer other academic opportunities such as having one-on-one tutoring sessions or group sessions with other students.
